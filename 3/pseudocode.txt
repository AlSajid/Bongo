Modular Design Pattern
________________________________

pseudo code:--------------

interface-

    Video Player

    controls
        Play
        Pause
        Rewind
        Forward

    import functionalities

--------
functionalities-

    Play
        play video
        disable play button
        enable pause button

    pause
        pause video
        enable play button
        disable pause button

    forward
        change video time +5 seconds

    rewind
        change video time - seconds
